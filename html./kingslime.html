King Slime

King Slime can be manually summoned at any place using the Slime Crown.

Crafting King Slime requires a Gold or Platinum crown (depending on what materials your world spawns) and twenty gel at a Demon or Crimson altar. Gel can be dropped by defeating slimes.

Recommended Weapons:

A ranged weapon, recommended Silver or Platinum bow with Frostburn or Flaming arrows. Since King Slime is slower, getting boots helps you distance yourself from him, letting you deal damage while staying safe. Furthermore, using a long platform to run on so you can kite out King Slime is ideal. If you have a lot of wood platforms, you can also set up an arena to run around, but you can run around in your world as shown in this video.
